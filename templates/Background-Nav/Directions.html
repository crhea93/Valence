Directions

  • Concepts
    • Place a Concept: Simply double click within outlined box labeled Cognitive Affective Map, enter information, and press enter
    • Update a Concept: Double click concept
    • Delete a Concept: Press small × belonging to the desired concept or simply select the concept and press either backspace or delete
  • Links
    • Place a Link: Click two concepts. They will become highlighted to indicated they are selected.
    • Update a Link: Click (or Control+ DoubleClick) a link to select it, press the enter, fill in the modal form and submit
    • Delete a Link: Click a link to select it, press either backspace or delete
  • Miscellaneous
    • Delete CAM: Select Delete CAM
